Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Kidora1?

Simply click "Sign Up" on our homepage and create your account. Choose your role (parent, child, volunteer, or donor) and start your journey with us.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption to protect your data. We never share personal information without your consent and comply with all data protection regulations.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! All basic features are available for free. Premium features require a subscription. Contact us to learn more about our pricing plans.

How can I become a volunteer?

Sign up on our platform and select the "Volunteer" role. You'll be guided through our volunteer onboarding process where you can specify your interests and availability.

How can I make a donation?

Log into your Kidora1 account and visit the "Donations" section. Choose a program or family you'd like to support and complete your secure transaction.

Do you offer support in multiple languages?

Currently, we support English, Yoruba, and Hausa. We're expanding language support regularly. Contact us if you need support in another language.
